How it works

  1. Order your kit

    Pick quantity and checkout. We ship your padded, prepaid mailer to your door.

  2. Pack & send

    Drop your devices in the mailer, seal, and hand to any carrier location. Tracking included.

  3. We triage responsibly

    Refurbishable items go to vetted partners. Others are harvested for parts or recycled.

  4. See your impact

    We show verified CO₂e savings and diversion metrics in your dashboard and email receipt.

FAQs

What can I send?
Phones, small tablets, wearables, chargers, and small accessories. Please remove SIM cards.
How do you handle my data?
We require factory resets and provide step-by-step guides. Downstream refurbishers perform certified wipes.
What happens to non-resalable devices?
Usable parts are harvested; the rest is recycled with certified partners. You’ll see this on your impact receipt.
When will my kit arrive?
Typically 3–5 business days. You’ll get a tracking link once it ships.
Do I get paid for my device?
Our D2C program focuses on convenience and sustainability. You pay for the kit; we handle responsible processing.
